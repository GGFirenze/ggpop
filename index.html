      GGpop and second-hand

      "If you don't use it, list it." That's the premise under which GGpop has grown into a community where millions of people buy and sell second-hand items every day. And we're only just getting started. Buying second-hand isn't just the best way to get what you need for a better price. Every time you buy something on GGpop, you promote more sustainable consumption because you extend the useful life of items and turn away from overproduction.
